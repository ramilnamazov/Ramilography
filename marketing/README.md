# Photography Marketing System

This is the operating system for growing the photography side hustle: brand,
website, content, organic social, paid ads, and the automation that ties it
together. It's designed so one person (you) + me (your AI agency/dev) can run
it without it eating your life.

## How this works

You are the **photographer and approver**. I am the **strategy + production +
ops** layer. You hand over assets, access, and approvals; I produce the work
and keep the engine running.

## Files in this folder

- `00-intake-brand-brief.md` — **Start here.** Fill this in. It's everything I
  need to know about you, your work, your customers, and your goals.
- `01-access-checklist.md` — The accounts and access I need, why I need each,
  and how to share safely.
- `roadmap.md` — The phased plan: what we build, in what order, and roughly when.
- `content/` — Where content calendars, captions, and copy will live.
- `assets/` — Where you drop logos, sample photos, and brand files (or a link
  to a shared drive).

## Ground rules I'll follow

- **Nothing goes live without your approval** until you explicitly tell me a
  channel is on autopilot.
- **You keep ownership** of every account, password, and ad-spend decision.
- **We start cheap.** Organic + free tools first; paid ads only once the
  website and tracking are ready to convert the traffic we pay for.
