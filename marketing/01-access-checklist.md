# Access Checklist — what I need access to, and why

**Golden rule: you stay the owner of everything.** Wherever possible, add me as
a *manager/editor*, not an owner. Never paste raw passwords into chat or files.
Use each platform's "invite a user/partner" feature, or a password manager's
secure share. If a platform has no delegate option, we'll set up a shared login
together and you can rotate the password anytime.

Legend: 🔴 needed to start · 🟡 needed soon · 🟢 nice to have later

---

## Identity & ownership (do this first)

- 🔴 **Confirm YOU are the owner** of: the website domain, Google Business
  Profile, Facebook Page, and Instagram account. If any are owned by an old
  developer/agency/ex-partner, we need to reclaim them before anything else.

## Google

- 🔴 **Google Business Profile** — add me as *Manager*. This is the single
  highest-ROI free asset for a local photographer. (Source of map results,
  reviews, posts.)
- 🟡 **Google Analytics 4 + Google Search Console** — so we can see traffic and
  what people search to find you. I can set these up if they don't exist.
- 🟢 **Google Ads** — only if we later test search ads.

## Website

- 🔴 **Website admin/editor access** (Wix/Squarespace/WordPress/etc.) — to
  optimize pages, speed, SEO, and add the inquiry form + tracking.
- 🔴 **Domain registrar access OR confirmation of who controls the domain** —
  needed for email, DNS, and to prove ownership.

## Meta (Facebook + Instagram) — this one has a specific right way

- 🔴 Make sure there's a **Meta Business Suite / Business Manager** account that
  *you* own, with the Facebook Page and Instagram connected to it.
- 🔴 Add me as a **Partner or Employee** in Business Manager with access to the
  Page, IG, and Ad Account. (Don't just make me an admin of the Page directly —
  Business Manager is cleaner and safer.)
- 🔴 **Ad Account** with a payment method *you* control. Spend always comes off
  your card; I never need your card number.
- 🟡 **Meta Pixel / Conversions API** — I'll create and install this on the site
  so ads can be measured and optimized. Without it, paid ads are flying blind.

## Content & scheduling

- 🟡 **A scheduling tool login** (we'll pick one — Meta Business Suite is free
  and enough to start; Later/Metricool/Buffer if we outgrow it).
- 🟢 **Canva** — I can generate and edit designs directly. Either share a
  Brand Kit or let me set one up.

## Email & follow-up

- 🟡 **An email tool** for newsletters/lead follow-up (start free:
  MailerLite/Brevo). I'll recommend and set up.
- 🟡 **The inbox where inquiries land** (or a forwarding rule) so I can help draft
  fast replies and templates. I can work with Gmail directly.

## Booking & reviews

- 🟢 **Booking/calendar tool** if you want online booking (Calendly/Acuity, or
  native to your site builder).
- 🟡 **Review collection** — we'll wire up a one-tap "leave a Google review" link.

---

## What I will NEVER need
- Your personal bank login.
- Your card numbers (payment methods are added by you, inside each platform).
- Ownership transfer of your accounts.

## Safe-sharing cheat sheet
- Google: Business Profile → Settings → *Managers* → add my email.
- Meta: business.facebook.com → *Business settings* → *Partners* / *People*.
- Website: most builders have *Contributors / Roles* under settings.
- Anything else: use a password manager's secure share link, set to expire.
